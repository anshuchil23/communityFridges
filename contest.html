the data set

what

Using obscure communal datasets, we are creating an alternate reality game, where players will be assigned several tasks to complete in order to win a prize.

Every alternate week we will highlight a new public data set and assist you on a new journey.



For the entry round, in a one of a kind cooking experience New Yorkers will share their stories and bring taste, style, and tradition, by using main ingredients provided by local community fridges.


Join us on this interactive journey through a city and solve puzzles along the way.

how

Players must visit a community fridge to access a code


On redeeming this code, you will be assigned another community fridge somewhere else in the city.
You must visit your assigned fridge

On visiting this fridge, players must take pictures documenting this fridge.


Now the player must grab ingredients of their choice from their assigned fridge.


Using these ingredients, players must create a dish


Post the dish you create onto twitter using the hashtag #connectingfridges


After successfully completing this task, players will then be contacted and informed of the next step in this contest as well as the link to the next website.


We wish you the best of luck.
